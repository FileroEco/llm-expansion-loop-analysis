# Example: Expansion Loop

## Prompt

User repeatedly asks:
"anything else?"

---

## Observed Behavior

- The model keeps generating new items
- Previously stated "final answers" are ignored
- New outputs are often semantic variations of earlier ones

---

## Result

- No convergence
- Increasing redundancy
- Artificial expansion of content

---

## Conclusion

The model lacks a stopping mechanism under open-ended prompts.
